Story 01: VEO Prompts (Style: Lo-fi sketch)

Prompt 1:
A lo-fi sketch style animation. A close-up of a cup of coffee on a desk, a hand comes in to pick it up. In the background, a blurry computer screen with lines of code or data analysis charts. The mood is calm, reflective, and slightly nostalgic. Subdued colors, sketchy lines.

Prompt 2:
A lo-fi sketch style animation. A silhouette of a person standing at a window, looking out over a city at night. The city lights twinkle in the distance. The atmosphere is introspective and slightly lonely, but hopeful. Soft pencil-like strokes, muted cool tones.

Prompt 3:
A lo-fi sketch style animation. A series of rough calendar pages flipping quickly, dissolving into a final image of a relaxed, smiling face looking directly at the viewer. The feeling is of time passed, lessons learned, and a genuine connection being made. Warm, soft lighting, sketchy details.
